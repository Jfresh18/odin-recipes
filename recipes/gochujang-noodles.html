Gochujang Noodles

Description

Spicy and saucy noodles in only 20 minutes!

Ingredients

Sauce ingredients

  • Soy sauce
  • Gochujang sauce/paste
  • Tomato paste
  • Peanut butter
  • Brown sugar
  • Sesame oil
  • Garlic
  • Broth or water

Noodle ingredients

  • Ground chicken
  • Ramen noodles
  • Spinach
  • Herbs, chili oil, and sesame seeds for toppings

Steps

  1. Whisk the sauce ingredients in a small bowl until thick and creamy.
  2. Get your ground chicken cooked in a skillet and season generously
  3. Cook the noodles until softened
  4. Get any extra toppings you want ready to add.
  5. Mix everything over heat to combine and add the extra broth as needed to add sauce.
