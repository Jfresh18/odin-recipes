Chili Crunch Tofu

Description

Simple baked tofu with a sweet & spicy chili sauce.

Ingredients

Tofu ingredients

  • 1 block extra firm tofu
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • a pinch of salt
  • 1/4 cup Yum Yum sauce
  • 1 tablespoon chili crisp
  • 1 tablespoon honey (optional)

Extras for serving

  • 1 1/2 cups rice, uncooked
  • 1/4 cup green onions for topping

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees
  2. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper
  3. Cut tofu into two even flat pieces. Press the moisture out of the tofu, using paper towels.
  4. Cut tofu into cubes and transfer to a bowl
  5. Add cornstarch, soy sauce, and olive oil. Toss gently to coat
  6. Cook rice however you want (see package instructions)
  7. Transfer the tofu to the baking sheet and roast for 20-25 minutes stirring once or twice during the process.
  8. Mix the tofu with several tablespoons of sauce, saving some for topping
  9. Serve on rice or whatever you prepared, add more sauce and green onions on top
